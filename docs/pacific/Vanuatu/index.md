## Altimetry

### Transition Altitude 

Within the Port Vila sector, the transition altitude is 11,000 ft (`A110`).

### Transition Level

| QNH (hPa) | Transition Level |
| --------- | ---------------- | 
| 980 Or Less | `F140` | 
| Greater than 980 | `F130` |
